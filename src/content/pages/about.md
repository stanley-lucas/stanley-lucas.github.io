I'm Stanley Lucas, a computer engineer based in Brazil with a deep interest in space systems and the engineering that makes them possible.

I build data engineering and backend infrastructure — the kind that makes complex systems observable and reliable. My current project is a [Falcon 9 Launch Telemetry Dashboard](https://github.com/stanley-lucas/falcon9-telemetry-dashboard): a full ETL pipeline that collects, processes, and visualizes historical SpaceX launch data.

Stack I work with: **Python · FastAPI · PostgreSQL · Docker · Streamlit**

## Why this blog

This blog is where I think out loud: architectural decisions, trade-offs, lessons from building real systems.

**Learning in public** — Writing forces clarity. If I can't explain an architectural decision in prose, I don't fully understand it yet. Every post here started as a question I had to answer for myself.

**Building a track record** — The decision logs, trade-off analysis, and benchmark posts here are my attempt to show how I think, not just what I can produce.
